# Hi there, I'm Onyks

I am a **Computer Science undergraduate** @ **University of Bologna** with experience in Competitive Programming and Italian Cybersecurity Olympiads.

Driven by a persistent **curiosity**, I spend my time exploring diverse architectural patterns and system-level security. I enjoy developing focused proofs-of-concept to investigate the spontaneous **ideas** that emerge as I navigate different areas of computer science ranging from distributed logic and decentralized monitoring to custom instruction sets and transparent networking.

I strongly believe that understanding a system's **failure** modes is as essential as its functionality. Here is why I am particularly interested in building local-first infrastructure and specialized tools that explore the boundaries of **operational security**.
